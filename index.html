A raid guide for the ascent through six corrupted encounters — Normal & Heroic

6 Bosses ilvl 246–269 Tier 35
Imperator Averzian
Grid Control · Void Tic-Tac-Toe · Add Denial
▾
The arena is a 3×3 grid. Averzian spawns three Voidshapers per wave that will claim a square if not stopped. Soak two of the three each wave so only one square is permanently claimed per round. If he ever completes three in a row, the raid instantly wipes to infinite adds.
Key Mechanics
Umbral Collapse — Marks 3 squares. Stack with the called marker to destroy that Voidshaper. You can only block 2 per wave — deliberately allow 1 non-threatening square to be claimed.
Dark Tears adds — Spawn in claimed squares. Assign 2 DPS to kill them fast. Tank must keep the boss away from active tears to prevent an empowerment buff.
Shatter — Tank cleave. Standard tank swap. Damage is steady but manageable on Normal.
Three squares in a row → March of the Endless. Call soak positions before every single wave.
Same Tic-Tac-Toe loop, but Averzian now applies simultaneous dispellable debuffs. If healers panic-dispel without coordination the overlapping explosions break grid positioning and cascade into missed soaks. Pre-assign a strict dispel order.
Heroic Additions
HeroicCoordinated dispels required — Simultaneous dispels cause overlapping raid-wide damage. Assign a strict dispel rotation — never reactive-cleanse.
HeroicHarder DPS cleanup — Dark Tears waves are more punishing. Delayed add kills directly threaten grid integrity.
Higher soak damage — Umbral Collapse hits harder. Ensure 3+ players soak each marked position so no one dies mid-wave.
Uncoordinated dispels fragment positioning → missed soaks → three-in-a-row. Lock in your dispel order before pulling.

Vorasius
Crystal Walls · Add Kiting · Healer Throughput Check
▾
Stationary boss that slams down crystal walls dividing the arena. A deadly sweeping Void Breath beam kills anyone behind standing walls. Kite Blistercreep adds into the walls to explode them. On Normal, one add breaks one wall. Healing ramps throughout as Primordial Roar stacks.
Key Mechanics
Crystal Walls — Spawn after Colossal Throw. Kite one Blistercreep into each wall to shatter it before Void Breath fires.
Void Breath — Sweeping beam. Everyone must be clear of standing walls. Assign 2 kite specialists for consistent add control.
Primordial Roar — Stacking raid damage. Rotate healing cooldowns throughout — don't save them all for late in the fight.
Smashing Frenzy — Tank must face it and take the hit, otherwise the raid takes a lethal area explosion.
Void Breath firing with walls still standing. 1 add = 1 wall on Normal. Time your kites carefully.
Same loop, but on Heroic it takes 2 Blistercreep deaths to break a single wall — not one. This doubles the add management workload. Primordial Roar ramps significantly faster. This is a genuine healer gear and cooldown-rotation check.
Heroic Additions
Heroic2 adds per wall — Each wall requires 2 Blistercreep deaths to shatter. Designate clear kiting pairs and lanes before pulling. Disorganised pathing causes failed breaks and wipes.
HeroicFaster Primordial Roar ramp — Healing requirements spike sharply in later phases. Assign externals to tanks and map every cooldown rotation before the pull.
Tighter DPS window — Reaching the third Void Breath with walls up is typically fatal. Plan cooldowns early enough to shorten the fight.
Kiting only 1 add onto a Heroic wall — it will not break. Pre-assign your kite pairs and communicate wall status in real time.

Fallen-King Salhadaar
Void Orbs · Staggered Kills · Interrupt Rotation · Burn Windows
▾
Two Concentrated Void orbs drift toward the boss. If either reaches him it is an instant wipe. Burn the orbs but kill them at least 8 seconds apart so Galactic Miasma DoT stacks don't overlap and overwhelm healers. The active tank must step away from the raid during Shattering Twilight to keep orb lanes clear.
Key Mechanics
Void Orbs — Assign 2 DPS per orb. Kill orb 1, wait 8 seconds for Galactic Miasma to fall off, then kill orb 2. Never overlap kills.
Fractured Image adds — Interrupt or stun on spawn. One dedicated interrupter per add is sufficient on Normal.
Cosmic Unraveling — At 100 energy the boss takes increased damage. Dump all cooldowns here.
Shattering Twilight — Active tank steps clear of the raid so spike patterns do not block orb lanes.
An orb reaching Salhadaar is an instant wipe. Orbs are always the absolute priority.
Orb management is the same but orb kills now apply Despotic Command, causing marked players to drop a spreading puddle when the debuff ends or is dispelled. Uncontrolled dispels flood the arena. Fractured Images on Heroic require a strict sequential kick order, not just reactive interrupts.
Heroic Additions
HeroicDespotic Command puddles — Marked players drop a puddle when the debuff expires or is dispelled. Stack puddles neatly at designated wall corners. Random early dispels destroy the arena floor.
HeroicSequential kick order — Fractured Images require a named interrupt rotation. One missed kick cascades into dangerous add casts that pressure healers during an orb kill.
Higher soak damage — Rotate defensive cooldowns on both orb DPS squads. Galactic Miasma hits harder.
Arena covered in Despotic Command puddles from uncontrolled dispels leaves no safe positions for orb paths. Assign dispel order before the pull.

Vaelgor & Ezzorak
Dual Dragons · HP Parity · Gloom Soaks · First Major Wall
▾
Two dragons that must stay more than 15 yards apart and die within 10% HP of each other — otherwise they gain 100% bonus damage until the imbalance is fixed. Vaelgor fires a targeted Dread Breath cone. Ezzorak fires Gloom orbs that 5 players must intercept on the way to shrink the resulting puddle. During intermission: stack in the Radiant Barrier and kill Manifestation of Midnight fast.
Key Mechanics
HP parity — Split DPS evenly. A dedicated caller watches HP percentages and redirects DPS if one dragon falls behind. Never exceed a 10% gap.
Dread Breath — Target steps the cone to the side, then gets dispelled. Never stand behind either dragon — Tail Lash will launch you.
Gloom orbs — Aim to blue marker. 5 ranged players soak on the path to shrink the puddle. Rotate soak teams every orb due to the 1-minute Diminish debuff.
Void Howl + Voidorbs — Stack tight so orbs spawn clustered. Snap Nullzone tethers fast (tank snaps last), then AoE down the Voidorbs immediately.
Intermission — Both dragons cast Midnight Flames. Stack in Radiant Barrier. Purple circle targets step to the side. Kill Manifestation of Midnight before it ramps.
HP imbalance over 10% triggers a 100% damage buff that overwhelms tanks in seconds. Use a designated parity caller and act early.
All the same mechanics, significantly more punishing. Voidorbs now free-cast Voidbolt if not immediately stunned — use Mass Grip and AoE burst. The Manifestation of Midnight in intermission gains 75% attack speed every 30 seconds it survives, making slows progressively useless. Kill it before the first 30-second mark.
Heroic Additions
HeroicVoidorbs free-cast — Unstunned orbs channel Voidbolt, dealing dangerous raid-wide damage. Stun them the moment Void Howl resolves. Mass Grip is extremely valuable here to cluster orbs for AoE.
HeroicManifestation ramps fast — Every 30 seconds: +75% attack speed and slows become 50% less effective. Kill it before the first 30-second mark or it becomes nearly unkillable.
Less margin on parity — Twilight Bond activates faster. The parity caller must be proactive — react to a 5% gap, not wait for 10%.
Unstunned Voidorbs free-casting while the intermission add ramps simultaneously is the most common pull-ender on Heroic. Lock in stuns and Mass Grip, kill the add fast.

Lightblinded Vanguard
Triple Council · Rotating Auras · Assignment-Heavy · Paladin Toolkit
▾
Three simultaneous paladin bosses: Lightblood (Retribution), Bellamy (Protection), and Senn (Holy). Each has an independent energy bar and applies a different raid-wide aura when full. Keep all three more than 40 yards apart — overlapping auras cause extreme amplification or full damage reduction. Burst during Lightblood. Survive Senn's pacification with pre-cast instants.
Aura Reference
Lightblood aura (offensive) — Raid gains a damage buff. Pop all DPS cooldowns immediately. This is your burst window.
Bellamy aura (defensive) — Boss damage reduced. Save cooldowns, let healers breathe.
Senn aura (pacification) — Raid-wide silence. Pre-cast all HoTs and instant-cast heals before it lands. Healers use instants only during this window.
Boss spacing — Keep all three more than 40 yards apart at all times. Assign tank positions before pulling.
Aura overlap from bosses drifting too close, or Senn's pacification landing on an unprepared raid.
The biggest Heroic trap: using Heroism/Bloodlust activates Divine Shield on two bosses, making them immune to damage until Mass Dispelled. Never lust without a Mass Dispel ready. Aura overlaps are lethal, not just punishing. Senn also casts Divine Toll, adding targeted silences on top of the pacification aura.
Heroic Additions
HeroicHeroism triggers Divine Shields — Lust/BL makes two bosses immune to all damage until Mass Dispelled. Most guilds save lust for after the immunity clears. Never lust without confirming a Mass Dispel is ready.
HeroicAura overlap is lethal — Bosses within 40 yards cause extreme damage amplification that kills players in the overlap zone. One drifting tank can wipe the raid instantly.
HeroicDivine Toll silences — Senn adds targeted silence mechanics. Assign specific players to interrupt Toll before it completes.
Lusting without Mass Dispel → two immune bosses → remaining boss energy auras stack → raid dies. Never lust without a plan.

Crown of the Cosmos
Alleria Windrunner · Multi-Phase · AotC & World First Boss
▾
Alleria Windrunner across three stages. Phase 1: tank all three Undying Sentinels immediately — each untanked sentinel stacks Echoing Darkness and kills the raid before the fight properly begins. Intermission: constant movement, avoid knockbacks. Phase 2: Gravity Collapse overlaps with Cosmic Anomalies — every cooldown must be planned before the pull.
Phase 1 — Undying Sentinels
Tank all three Sentinels immediately — The single most common cause of early wipes on Normal. All three must be grabbed the moment they spawn or Echoing Darkness stacks rapidly wipe the raid.
Silverstrike Arrow — Navigate through the correct cleansing line. It removes debuffs but applies a brief healing reduction. Don't chain multiple players into the same line simultaneously.
Void Zones — Persistent and expanding throughout all phases. Manage spacing constantly.
Intermission
Grasp of Emptiness — Beam explosions requiring constant movement. Avoid knockback floor hazards. Use raid-wide cooldowns here to ease the transition into Phase 2.
Phase 2 — Crown of the Cosmos
Gravity Collapse + Cosmic Anomalies — The primary coordination check. Both mechanics occur in Phase 2. Execute both cleanly with planned cooldowns or the raid dies to combined pressure.
Most common: failing to grab all 3 Sentinels in Phase 1. Second most common: entering Phase 2 with no cooldowns available.
All Phase 1 mechanics are more severe. Silverstrike now applies Null Corona healing absorbs — dispelling incorrectly causes the absorb to jump to nearby players. Phase 2 Alleria's damage ramps to lethal far faster. Gravity Collapse and Cosmic Anomaly overlap simultaneously with no margin for error. This is the AotC fight. Map every cooldown before you pull.
Heroic Additions
HeroicNull Corona absorbs — Silverstrike Arrows apply a large healing absorb. Incorrectly dispelling it causes the absorb to jump to adjacent players. Assign a named dispel order — no reactive mass-cleansing.
HeroicPhase 2 ramp is lethal — Alleria's damage increases much faster. Entering Phase 2 with cooldowns spent from sloppy Phase 1 usage is almost always fatal. Preserve them.
HeroicSimultaneous overlap — Gravity Collapse and Cosmic Anomalies spawn at the same time. Assign movement lanes and soak positions for this exact moment before the pull.
Dedicated orb team required — Winging orb interception on Heroic ends at 1 kill and many wipes. Assign a dedicated 4–5 player orb group before the first pull.
Phase 2 Gravity Collapse + Anomaly overlap with depleted raid cooldowns. This is where most guilds spend their Heroic progression. Map every cooldown. AotC requires her death on Heroic difficulty.

Chimaerus the Undreamt God
Dual Realm · Add Control · Multi-Phase · Burn Windows
▾
Chimaerus exists in two realms simultaneously. Manifestations spawn in the Aln (spirit realm) invisible to normal players — only those hit by Alndust Upheaval can see and kill them. Kill Manifestations before they breach into Reality and reach Chimaerus, or he eats them and gains stacking damage buffs. At 100 energy he flies and drops Corrupted Devastation beams; land his Ravenous Dive return with zero surviving Manifestations.
Key Mechanics
Alndust Upheaval — Tank-targeted slam that knocks nearby players up and grants them Alnsight for 40 sec. Alnsight-players can see and damage Manifestations in the Aln. Stack near the tank to guarantee coverage each cast.
Rift Emergence / Coalesced Manifestations — Three types emerge: Colossal Horror (tank, spams Discordant Roar — escalating raid damage, kick on cooldown), Haunting Essence (healer, casts Fearsome Cry fear — stun or dispel), Swarming Shade (DPS target, no CC). Prioritise: Haunting Essence → Colossal Horror → Swarming Shade.
Alnshroud — Each Manifestation has a damage shield. Only Alnsight players can strip it. Once removed the Manifestation bursts into Reality and walks toward Chimaerus — kill it immediately.
Insatiable / Cannibalized Essence — Any Manifestation that reaches Chimaerus in Reality grants him +10% damage and heals him for 500% of the add's remaining HP. Even one stack is punishing; zero adds reaching him is the core execution requirement.
Caustic Phlegm — Periodic raid-wide DoT on all players. Healers should save throughput cooldowns for this window, especially when Rift Sickness absorb stacks are high.
Rending Tear — Frontal cone on current tank target. Face Chimaerus away from raid at all times. Tank swap after each cast to manage the bleed stack.
Stage Two: Corrupted Devastation — At 100 energy Chimaerus takes to the skies. He sweeps beams across the platform — move through each beam path after it fires to avoid doubling back into the next sweep. Surviving Manifestations break into Reality during this phase. Kill them before Ravenous Dive lands.
Ravenous Dive — Chimaerus slams back down, consuming all remaining Manifestations still alive. Zero adds = zero stacks. This is the burn check: every Manifestation alive when he lands is a permanent damage buff on the boss for the rest of the fight.
Wipe Conditions
Chimaerus reaches 8–10 stacks of Cannibalized Essence — incoming damage becomes unmitigable.
Healer collapse from compounding Rift Sickness absorbs + Caustic Phlegm with no cooldowns available.
Multiple uninterrupted Discordant Roar casts — each one ramps raid damage by 10%; 4–5 uninterrupted casts is usually fatal.
Same dual-realm structure but significantly tighter. Consuming Miasma is added — targeted players build a ticking AoE that erupts on removal, consuming nearby Alndust Essence puddles and knocking everyone back. Dispel timing is critical. Rift Shroud makes Chimaerus immune during Stage Two unless broken. Ravenous Dive no longer clears Alndust Essence puddles, so Stage Two positioning becomes increasingly dangerous over multiple cycles.
Heroic Additions
Consuming Miasma — Targeted players suffer escalating Nature damage for 1 min. On dispel (or natural expiry), erupts with Lingering Miasma: 10-yard knockback + DoT on nearby players, and consumes Alndust Essence puddles in the area. Dispel away from other players and away from Stage Two puddle clusters.
Rift Shroud — During Stage Two, Chimaerus gains a 99% damage reduction shield. DPS must burn Manifestations with Alnsight to strip the shroud before dealing meaningful boss damage. Coordinate Alnsight coverage across the squad — you need minimum 5–6 players with the buff active simultaneously to strip it quickly.
Ravenous Dive no longer clears puddles — Alndust Essence zones from Heroic Corrupted Devastation persist through the dive. The arena floor fills progressively each cycle; route your movement early and keep melee aware of safe zones.
Alnshroud Burst — On Heroic, removing Alnshroud leaves an Alndust Essence puddle at the Manifestation's position. Kill adds away from the centre and away from designated movement corridors.
Wipe Conditions
Failing to strip Rift Shroud during Stage Two — boss becomes unkillable while healers are overwhelmed by Manifestation pressure.
Consuming Miasma dispelled into the raid or into puddle clusters — chain knockbacks make the platform unplayable.
Puddle accumulation blocking melee and ranged positions by cycle 3+ — healers cannot reach players, tanks cannot kite Manifestations safely.

Dungeon Rotation
Magisters' Terrace
4 Bosses · Arcane Hazards · Energy Soak
▾
Dungeon Bonus
Early in the run, interact with an Arcane Tome in the library — grants Arcane Empowerment (+5% Haste for 30 min, persists through death).
Boss Tips
Arcanotron
Soak Energy Orbs during Refueling Protocol to stop the boss consuming them — each orb left grants it a group-wide AoE blast. Soaking stacks Unstable Energy, so spread the soaks. Boss takes 20% increased damage during this phase — burn hard. TANK: Use defensives on Repulsing Slam. HEAL: Dispel Ethereal Shackles immediately.
Seranel Sunlash
Stand inside the Suppression Zone when you have Runic Mark — entering it triggers Feedback and clears the debuff while blocking Wave of Silence silence. Null Reaction stacks make dodging harder so don't linger. TANK: If party can't dispel Hastening Ward, pop major defensives.
Gemellus
Copies share health — hit all of them. At 50% HP a second Triplicate occurs. If you have Neural Link, hug the matching copy to remove its shield. During Astral Grasp use any movement speed to escape the Cosmic Radiation aura around the boss.
Degentrius (Final)
Spread players in the pizza-slice positions around the boss — you must bounce the Unstable Void ball between your positions. Missing it gives a stacking Void Destruction debuff (2+ stacks = wipe). Never touch the Void Torrent beam boundaries. TANK: Manage Umbral Splinters drops carefully — the Stygian Ichor is painful for DPS to walk through.
Maisara Caverns
3 Bosses · Dual Kill Sync · Necromancer Theme
▾
Boss Tips
Muro'jin & Nekraxx
Kill both within seconds of each other — surviving one triggers Bestial Wrath / Revive Pet. Use defensives when you have Infected Pinions, especially if Barrage is targeting you simultaneously. Enter Nekraxx's Freezing Trap if Carrion Swoop marks you. TANK: Stack both bosses for cleave. Use active mitigation on Flanking Spear.
Vordaza
When Unstable Phantoms spawn with Final Pursuit, force them to collide with each other rather than attacking them directly (99% damage reduction). Each collision = Lingering Dread stack + ground pool, so keep stacks below 2. Save offensive cooldowns for Necrotic Convergence phase — the shield ramps damage the longer it holds. TANK: Major defensive on each Drain Soul.
Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls (Final)
During Crush Souls, the boss targets 3 players — stack close to improve Soulbind Totem cleave. Sidestep Volatile Essence puddles. During Soulrending Roar, CC Malignant Souls to gain Spectral Residue stacking buff (makes the boss die faster). TANK: Position boss near Soulbind Totem for cleave. Use defensives on Spiritbreaker.
Nexus-Point Xenas
3 Bosses · Leyline Mechanics · Void/Light Duality
▾
Boss Tips
Chief Corewright Kasreth
Never cross an active Leyline Array — lethal without defensives. When you have Reflux Charge, destroy as many arrays as possible. Outrange Corespark Detonation, then avoid the Arcane Spill afterburn. Interrupt Arcane Zap whenever possible. HEAL: Plan cooldowns after each Corespark Detonation — the Sparkburn DoT follows immediately.
Corewarden Nysarra
Focus the Dreadflail and Grand Nullifier when Null Vanguard activates — must be dead before Lightscar Flare. During Lightscar Flare the boss takes 300% increased damage, so save all major DPS cooldowns. TANK: Major defensives on Umbral Lash — the Void Gash follow-up hits hard.
Lothraxion (Final)
During Divine Guile, identify the real Lothraxion by his horns — interrupt the shade. Wrong interrupt triggers Core Exposure (massive raid damage). Use defensives if targeted by Brilliant Dispersion — the follow-up DoT is brutal and creates Fractured Images that knock back on contact.
Windrunner Spire
4 Bosses · Wind Mechanics · Dual Kill Sync
▾
Boss Tips
Emberdawn
Place Flaming Updraft near a corner to give your team space to dodge Flaming Twisters. During Burning Gale channel, watch for Fire Breath frontals. TANK: Keep boss in a corner. Use major defensives on Searing Beak. HEAL: Major cooldowns during Burning Gale channel.
Derelict Duo (Kalis & Latch)
Kill both simultaneously — surviving one triggers Broken Bond enrage. The only way to stop Kalis's Debilitating Shriek is to use Latch's Heaving Yank to hook Kalis back. During Curse of Darkness, outrange the Dark Entity if you can't dispel it. Place Gunk Splatter puddles safely. TANK: Active mitigation on each Bone Hack.
Commander Kroluk
Stack together during Intimidating Shout to avoid fear. At 66% and 33% HP, boss casts Rallying Bellow (adds spawn + boss goes immune) — kill adds ASAP. DPS the boss only once adds are dead. Bait Reckless Leap as the furthest player and use defensives. TANK: Major defensives on Rampage.
The Restless Heart (Final)

Ranjit
Wind Chakram kills — never let it hit you. During Chakram Vortex, dodge the Gale Surge waves simultaneously. Position carefully before each Gale Surge — knockback + Coalesced Wind orb spawn. Use defensives on Fan of Blades bleed. TANK: Move boss away from returning Chakram so melee don't die.

Soak the solar beams to prevent Araknath from gaining Solar Infusion stacks — too many stacks leads to a lethal Supernova. Dodge Defensive Protocol heat waves. Use defensives when soaking (damage is real). Stay in melee range to suppress Blast Wave. TANK: Keep Fiery Smash frontal away from team.
Rukhran

Forgemaster Garfrost
When targeted by Throw Saronite, place Ore Chunks close together so the tank can break them all with one Orebreaker charge. Both bosses share health via Necrolink — always cleave both. Interrupt Krick's Death Bolt. During Shade Shift, focus Shades of Krick and interrupt their Shadowbind (it's a Curse — decurse works). If you get Lumbering Fixation stay far from the boss. TANK: Stack both bosses at all times. Major defensives on Blight Smash.

Place Rime Blast debuff on top of Bone Piles / Infused Bone Piles — this dramatically reduces the add count during Army of the Dead. During Army of the Dead, interrupt Scourge Plaguespreader at all costs. Avoid Death's Grasp puddles and Ice Barrage. TANK: Major defensives on Scourgelord's Brand. Avoid Scourgelord's Reckoning.
